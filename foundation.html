Wine Spectator
Scholarship Foundation

Education has always been a primary goal of Wine Spectator. Proceeds from the Wine Experience, now in its 34th year, support the Wine Spectator Scholarship Foundation, which provides grants and scholarships to a wide variety of wine-related and culinary programs.

To date, more than $20 million has been raised. This extraordinary sum has been made possible by the generosity of vintners from around the world, who donate the wine for the Wine Experience Grand Tastings and seminars, as well as the thousands of wine lovers who attend the event each year.

Wine Spectator is proud to be a contributor to the following programs, and others that advance the development of America's wine and hospitality industries.

Among the many beneficiaries:

Sonoma State University: $3 million to construct a new home for SSU's Wine Business Institute, to be called
the Wine Spectator Learning Center

Florida International University Hospitality School: $1 million to support the Wine Spectator Restaurant
Management Lab

University of California, Davis: $3.6 million to the Department of Viticulture and Enology for scholarships. To date we have awarded 622 scholarships. The 2014 recipients:

Wine Spectator Greystone Restaurant, CIA, St. Helena, CA

Wine Spectator Tasting Room, CIA, Hyde Park, NY

Cornell University: School of Hotel Administration scholarship endowment


University of Miami: School of Communication scholarship for wine journalism


University of California, Berkeley: California Wine Pioneers Oral Histories, Bancroft Library


American Institute of Wine and Food


Society of Wine Educators


James Beard Foundation

and others...
